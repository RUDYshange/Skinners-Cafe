About Skinner

Nestled in the vibrant heart of Braamfontein, Skinner Cafe is more than just a coffee shop – it's a cultural hub where students, creatives, and professionals gather to fuel their passions.

Our carefully curated menu features premium single-origin coffees, artisanal pastries, and healthy meal options that celebrate both local flavors and international cuisine. From sunrise espressos to late-night study sessions, we're here to energize your journey.

Experience the pulse of Johannesburg's most dynamic neighborhood, right here at the corner of Juta and De Beer Street.
